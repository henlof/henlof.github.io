About the site

This site contains original genealogical research about the Swedish farm Per Nirs, dating back to 1607. The sources are mainly original documents and historical literature.

All texts are written by Bengt Löf, who is a descendent of the Per Nirs founder.

The website was produced by Pontus Vinderos.
